About Gorillaz

Gorillaz is a British virtual band created in 1998 by musician Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett. The band consists of four animated members: 2-D, Murdoc Niccals, Noodle, and Russel Hobbs. Known for their unique blend of music styles and innovative visual presentations, Gorillaz has released several successful albums and singles over the years.
