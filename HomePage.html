About Gorillaz

Gorillaz is a British virtual band created in 1998 by musician Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett. The band consists of four animated members: 2-D, Murdoc Niccals, Noodle, and Russel Hobbs. Known for their unique blend of music styles and innovative visual presentations, Gorillaz has released several successful albums and singles over the years.

Featured Review

Demon Days (2005), Album Review: Gorillaz's second studio album "Demon Days," released in 2005, is widely regarded as a masterpiece. The album features hit singles like "Feel Good Inc." and "DARE," showcasing the band's ability to blend genres such as alternative rock, hip-hop, and electronic music. The dark themes and innovative production have earned it critical acclaim and a lasting legacy in the music world.
